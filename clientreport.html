General Experience and Reflection

I think the most challenging part of this project, was having to communicate effectively with everyone and try to understand what they wanted me to make. I went out of my way to be more talkative and figure out what they wanted from me.
I think the part that I enjoyed the most was to be able to see the site plan and then be able to make it happen, especially when I had never coded before the semester started.
If I was to do anything differently, I think I would have done everything more spread out than trying to do everything at once.
I feel a lot more confident now that I know the basics and then can use other tools like AI to make more complicated things.
